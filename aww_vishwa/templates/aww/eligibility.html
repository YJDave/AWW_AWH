ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR ANGANWADI WORKER
  • Anganwadi worker should be daughter-in-law of that village/ward and acceptable in the local community.
  • Female candidate of age 18 to 45 years.
  • Candidates who have completed 10th or its equivalent qualification from a recognized Board/University are eligible to apply for ICDS Anganwadi Worker.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR ANGANWADI HELPER
  • Anganwadi helper should be daughter-in-law of that village/ward and acceptable in the local community.
  • Female candidate of age 18 to 45 years.
  • Candidates who have completed 7th or its equivalent qualification from a recognized Board/University are eligible to apply for ICDS Anganwadi.
