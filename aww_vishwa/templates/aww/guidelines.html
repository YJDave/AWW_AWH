How to use our website/system ,and apply ?

I. For Applicants:
First,Select your profession
Then fill the related application form.
Select the agree button, if you are agree with all the rules.
Finally, click on Submit button.
It will show the popup box "Form submitted successfully!"

II. For Admin:
First get logged in the admin account with your username and password.
You will be able to see applicants detail, see digital marking of applicants, select applicant from apply list.
You will be able to send automatic call or automatic message for selected applicants.
Logout from your account successfully.

How does the process work, after registrations ?
First step is digital marking based on your qualification,marriage certificate,locality and some other factors.
Then, selection based on digital marking is performed.
Selected cantidate will get a call or message for the interview.
Interview of selected cantidates in front of three officer of Government.
Selected cantidate for the job will recieve final selection call for their job.
Then they will get offer letter for their job.
